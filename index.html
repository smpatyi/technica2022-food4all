First and foremost, our mission is to connect college students experiencing food insecurity to safe, nutritious, and reliable food sources. We believe that everyone deserves food security. You might be surprised to learn that anywhere from 20% to 50% of college students experience food insecurity. When a person doesn’t have reliable access to nutritious food options, they are impacted every way possible: physically, socially, emotionally, and academically. Read the full article from Drexel University’s Center for Hunger-Free Communities >
Whenever possible, we aim to incorporate methods and solutions that work to eliminate food waste in the United States. According to the United Nations, one-third of all food produced in the world is wasted. And, did you know that the United States discards more food than any other country in the world? We toss nearly 40 million tons (80 billion pounds) every year, which makes up 30% to 40% of our entire food supply. Read the full article from RTS >
